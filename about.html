Sophie Cundy

The Artist & Mother

I’ve had the opportunity to photograph families and weddings since 2011. Recently, I relocated from Dallas, Texas to the peaceful countryside of South Gloucestershire. This year, I’m launching my photography business in the UK, serving couples and families across Gloucestershire, Bristol, and Bath — while continuing to offer fall sessions in Dallas–Fort Worth each October.

As someone who values being present and emotional depth, I specialize in creating timeless, refined imagery filled with soul. My work is designed for couples who cherish artistry, legacy, and genuine connection.

My approach is very individualized. No client is just a client, but perhaps a bride looking to carve out an important part of her story, a child looking to be understood, a sibling wanting a voice, or a parent holding moments in time… all beautifully human.
