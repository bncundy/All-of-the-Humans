The Experience

01. Connect

We start with a conversation, not a contract. Let's see if our vibes align.

Before a camera is ever lifted, we begin with a consultation designed to see if our perspectives on storytelling align. This isn't just a transactional exchange; it’s where we determine if my documentary style is the right fit for your family's unique magnetism. We’ll discuss the honest texture of your daily dynamic—whether your home is loud and chaotic or quiet and cuddly—moving past the formal contract to establish a genuine collaboration. By the time we "close the deal," you will feel a sense of ease knowing that your unscripted legacy is in hands that value presence over performance.

45 min session, or 90 min for extended families

02. Create

No stiff posing. Just you, your people, and a space to be yourselves.

On the day of the session, the pressure to "perform" for the camera disappears entirely. Whether we are in the comfort of your own home or outdoors in the South Gloucestershire countryside, I am there to document what I observe through my own point of view. You can expect a relaxed environment where we move at your family's pace—capturing the messy breakfast faces, the quiet cuddles, and the honest texture of your daily life. By creating a space where you can simply be yourselves, we capture unique, comfortable photos that feel exactly like the season you are in.

25+ fully edited images & custom corrections

03. Keep

Heirlooms delivered digitally and in print, ready to outlast us all.

Once our time together concludes, I meticulously edit your gallery to ensure the imagery remains artful, stripped-down, and raw. You are provided with a curated collection of high-resolution digital and print-ready heirlooms that are designed to outlast the current moment. These images serve as a tangible record of your beautiful connectivity, moving beyond mere files to become a preserved legacy. You’ll walk away with an honest record of your life—moments edited with care and intended to be kept forever.

Starting from £320 or $450
